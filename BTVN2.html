Black Myth : Wukong

Black Myth: Wukong's first trailer in 2020. Boasting jaw-dropping visuals and flashy, action-packed combat, Black Myth: Wukong immediately drew me in with its gritty interpretation of the classic Chinese novel Journey to the West

This isn't really about Wukong himself, or the trickster's actions through the ancient writings, but instead you take up the mantle of his successor of sorts. You play as a young, voiceless monkey many years after the fall of the god.

chipbozz - Positive "Black Myth: Wukong" is undoubtedly the most anticipated game of 2024, marking a groundbreaking moment for Chinese AAA single-player games. MacKong - Positive This game, initially touted as an "action RPG," is actually much closer to a Souls-like, with challenging combat that requires precision, especially in dodging. While it has unique mechanics, like building energy for heavy attacks, the difficulty can be punishing, which may surprise players expecting a more traditional RPG experience. The Chinese mythological setting is beautifully presented, with AAA-quality cutscenes and stunning Unreal 5 visuals, although the world is quite linear. Despite some minor flaws, it's an impressive first release that may rank among the top games of the year—just be prepared for a tough challenge. 9/10
