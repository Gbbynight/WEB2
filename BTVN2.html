Mortal Kombat

Cole Young, an MMA fighter accustomed to taking beatings for money, is unaware of his heritage or why Outworld's Emperor Shang Tsung has sent Sub-Zero, a deadly Cryomancer, to hunt him down. Fearing for his family's safety, Cole seeks out Sonya Blade with the help of Jax, a Special Forces Major who shares the same mysterious dragon marking as Cole. This search leads him to the temple of Lord Raiden, the Elder God and protector of Earthrealm, where he joins forces with warriors Liu Kang, Kung Lao, and rogue mercenary Kano.

As Cole trains alongside these experienced fighters, he prepares to face the looming threat from Outworld in a battle for the universe. But to stand a chance, Cole must unlock his arcana, a powerful ability hidden deep within his soul. The stakes are high—not just for his family, but for the fate of Earthrealm itself.
